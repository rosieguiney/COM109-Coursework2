About our brand

StressLess was made by students, for students. We understand deeply the stress that comes with coursework, exams and deadlines in the university experience. We feel that personal health and wellbeing matter most when navigating these challenges in our everyday lives, which is why we created this service to improve your wellbeing.

Who we are:

StressLess is a student-focused health and wellbeing site, created to tackle the pressures of academic life that students face, because no student should feel overwhelmed when trying to achieve their university degree.

We provide users with evidence-based knowledge, coping tools and techniques, and external resources for full support. We know your mental health journey is personal, that’s why we uphold confidentiality and transparency to ensure there is no hidden tracking and no selling of your data to 3rd parties.
